Billy Tran

Billy Tran is a current BA Graphic Design student at San Jose State University. He often creates artistic pieces along with designs. Drawing people and creating animal pieces is his specialty, not focusing with just one one medium always trying new techniques and new things. Billy is a artist from New York that moved to California to further his experience and skills of art and design further.
